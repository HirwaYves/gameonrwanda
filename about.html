Our Story

Game On Rwanda was founded in 2022 with a simple yet powerful vision: to transform the workplace culture in Rwanda by bringing the unifying power of sports to institution workers across the country.

What started as a small pilot tournament with just 5 institutions has grown into Rwanda's premier workplace sports organization, serving over 25 institutions and 500+ participants annually.

Our journey began when a group of passionate sports enthusiasts working in various institutions recognized the need for a platform that would not only promote physical activity but also foster teamwork, leadership, and community building among professionals.

Our Mission

To foster teamwork, camaraderie, and healthy competition among Rwandan institution workers through inclusive sports programs that promote physical fitness, mental well-being, and professional networking.

Our Vision

To become the leading workplace sports organization in East Africa, creating a model for how sports can transform workplace culture and build stronger, more connected professional communities.

Our Values

  • Inclusion: Everyone has a place in our sports community
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in everything we do
