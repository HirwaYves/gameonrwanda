Our Mission

Game On Rwanda is committed to creating inclusive sports opportunities for all institution workers, regardless of their background, skill level, or physical abilities. Our programs are designed to promote teamwork, health, and community building across Rwanda's diverse workplace landscape.

Our Programs

Workplace Football League

Our flagship program featuring competitive football tournaments for institution workers. Teams compete in seasonal leagues with professional refereeing and facilities.

  • Seasonal tournaments
  • Professional coaching
  • Mixed gender teams
  • Championship finals

Youth Development Program

Specialized programs for young professionals (18-25) to develop leadership skills through sports while building career networks.

  • Leadership training
  • Mentorship programs
  • Career networking
  • Skill development
Learn More

Women's Empowerment League

Dedicated programs to encourage female participation in workplace sports, breaking barriers and promoting gender equality.

  • Female-only tournaments
  • Confidence building
  • Role model speakers
  • Support networks

Adaptive Sports Initiative

Inclusive sports programs designed for workers with disabilities, ensuring everyone can participate in workplace sports activities.
